Welcome to Survey Says!



Welcome to Survey Says, a Family Feud style game you can play on your own, without a family. Players will have 30 seconds to respond with the top six survey answers to a survey question. If you get three wrong answers or run out of time before responding with the top six survey answers, the game is over. We don't have Steve Harvey, but we do think you will enjoy this game. Have fun!
